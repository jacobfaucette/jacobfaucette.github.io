Subject: XK-37-10

Project Status: Success

Description:

XK-37-10 is the culmination of the greatest minds to find a way to protect our home. It is a self-replicating army of Warforged equipped with the tools to drive back The Great March. The hollow shells of the machines will house the souls of the brave soldiers that gave their life to the cause. Each Warforged stands at six feet tall and weighs over seven-hundred pounds with armor strong enough that enemy blades to shatter upon striking it.

Project Notes:

The Empire has show great interest in this project and it has resurrected. their hope on winning the war. The funding of the other research projects have been either reduced or gutted completely, and has been allocated to XK-37-10.
Dr. Chaymond has requested that funding not be cut from the other projects, but this motion has been denied.
