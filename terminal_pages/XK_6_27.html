Subject: XK-6-27

Project Status: Success

Description:

XK-6-27 is the fusion and binding of a soul of an Angel with a Warforged shell. This process allows for a greater level of adapability from the Warforged as the bound soul and the underlying programming allow for continous artifical neural network enhancements to be integrated seamlessly. The Angel soul trends towards improving peaceful combat resolution capabilities, but other improvements have been documented.
